 PRODUCT HIGHLIGHT 

 Lukka Crypto Benchmarks Trusted Solutions for Digital Asset Benchmarking

 Proprietary and Redistributed Data :  Tailored to meet diverse use cases with custom data solutions. 

 Pre-Established and Custom Benchmarks :  For enhanced flexibility in tracking market performance.

 AML Compliance and Risk Reporting :  Ensuring transparency and security for tokenized assets.

 About Lukka
