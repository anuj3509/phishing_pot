Critical Security Update for Your Ledger Device Firmware Update: Security Incident We are writing to inform you of a security incident involving our infrastructure that requires your immediate action to ensure the safety of your assets. Our security team recently discovered that a threat actor gained unauthorized access to a specific server within our Ledger Live backend infrastructure. During this breach, the attacker successfully deployed malware onto the compromised server. The malware was designed to target the communication protocol between Ledger devices and our servers. While the server-side vulnerability has been neutralized and the compromised infrastructure has been rebuilt, the nature of this breach creates a potential risk for devices that haven't updated their firmware yet. To eliminate this risk, it is critically important that all users update their device's firmware immediately. This mandatory update contains a new security patch that hardens the device’s protocols and renders the previous attack vector completely ineffective. What You Must Do Now Your assets are at risk until this update is completed. Please perform the following steps: Ensure you have the latest version of the Ledger Live application.
